Key Flood Impacts

  • Inundation of low-lying residential areas
  • Damage to roads, houses, and local infrastructure
  • Displacement of people into relief camps
  • Rescue operations using boats and emergency teams
  • Disruption to transport, livelihood, and daily activities
  • Flood relief distribution to affected communities
